## Transferring styles to the 5th generation

At the moment, there are several generations of the Payment Widget design available for the clients of Platform. For newly onboarded clients, as a rule, we offer the 5th generation of the payment form design for use in their projects. For projects configured with the use of earlier Payment Widget generations, there is a gradual process of transition to the payment form with the 5th generation design. If the merchant is ready to be upgraded, they are advised to contact the Platform technical support. Otherwise, they can wait till they are contacted by Platform and agree on the convenient timeframe for the upgrade.
