# Reporting positions

Statement information is itemised with the help of reporting positions: for total amounts of different balances in each currency (as reflected in the brief summary) and for data contained in each separate balance (as reflected in the list of balances). In addition, different pricing models require the use of different kinds of reporting positions for itemisation of data. The Interchange++ pricing model relies on a wider range of reporting positions, hence, when working with these, keep in mind the following:

The following table contains a full list of reporting positions used.

| Position | Balance types | Description |
| --- | --- | --- |
| Waiting for info | `INOUTIN/OUT` | Amount of funds awaiting clearing (settling with the card networks following the reconciliation of operations). As a rule, funds are considered waiting during the two days from the moment when the operation is considered complete.This item is relevant only for the Interchange++ pricing model when the reporting period includes the day of creating the report or the day before it. The position is not shown in the consolidated balance if its value equals zero. |
| Opening balance | `INOUTIN/OUT` | The total amount of funds available at the beginning of the specified time period and equal to the closing balance of the day before the specified time period |
| Pay-in volume | `ININ/OUT` | `salecapturerecurring`Amount of cash inflow from processing purchases (operations,,) |
| Pay-out volume | `OUTIN/OUT` | `payout`Amount of cash outflow from issuing payouts (operations) |
| Refund volume | `ININ/OUT` | Amount of cash outflow from refunding purchases processed earlier |
| Interchange | `INOUTIN/OUT` | Amount of funds charged for issuers' card payments processing costs. Relevant for the Interchange++ pricing model.When specifying the time period, keep in mind that:In those cases when values of all interchange positions (Interchange, Interchange scheme fee, and Interchange processor fee) are equal to zero, then they are not shown in the consolidated balance.If the time period does not include the day when the report is created and the day before it, then the fee amount is reflected in full.If the time period includes the day when the report is created or the day before it, then the fee amount may not be reflected in full (as some of the operations can still be pending).If the day of creating the report is set as the target time period, then this position, as well as other interchange positions, is not shown (since fee calculation takes place later). |
| Interchange scheme fee | `INOUTIN/OUT` | Amount of funds charged for processing costs of Mastercard and Visa according to their rules and regulations. Relevant for the Interchange++ pricing model.When specifying the time period, keep in mind that:In those cases when values of all interchange positions (Interchange, Interchange scheme fee, and Interchange processor fee) are equal to zero, then they are not shown in the consolidated balance.If the time period does not include the day when the report is created and the day before it, then the fee amount is reflected in full.If the time period includes the day when the report is created or the day before it, then the fee amount may not be reflected in full (as some of the operations can still be pending).If the day of creating the report is set as the target time period, then this position, as well as other interchange positions, is not shown (since fee calculation takes place later). |
| Interchange processor fee | `INOUTIN/OUT` | Amount of funds charged by Platform (as an acquirer) for processing card operations. Relevant for the Interchange++ pricing model.When specifying the time period, keep in mind that:In those cases when values of all interchange positions (Interchange, Interchange scheme fee, and Interchange processor fee) are equal to zero, then they are not shown in the consolidated balance.If the time period does not include the day when the report is created and the day before it, then the fee amount is reflected in full.If the time period includes the day when the report is created or the day before it, then the fee amount may not be reflected in full (as some of the operations can still be pending).If the day of creating the report is set as the target time period, then this position, as well as other interchange positions, is not shown (since fee calculation takes place later). |
| Chargeback volume | `ININ/OUT` | Amount of cash outflow from issuing chargebacks |
| Chargeback fee | `ININ/OUT` | Amount of funds charged for processing chargebacks received over the specified time period |
| Chargeback additional fee | `ININ/OUT` | The additional fee charged for the chargeback process in cases when the chargeback amount exceeds the established threshold |
| Retrieval request fee | `ININ/OUT` | Amount of funds charged for filing requests to retrieve additional information about processed payments (collaboration or retrieval requests) |
| Representment | `ININ/OUT` | Amount of cash inflow from processing successfully disputed chargebacks |
| Other service fees | `INOUTIN/OUT` | Amount of funds charged in accordance with the terms and conditions as part of other administrative fees, including chargeback arbitration fees |
| Funds transfer from/to balance | `INOUTIN/OUT` | Amount resulting from funds transfer which includes adding funds from other balances and settlement accounts and withdrawing funds to other balances |
| Transfer fee | `OUTIN/OUT` | Amount of funds charged for balance transfer |
| Currency exchange | `INOUTIN/OUT` | Amount resulting from transferring the funds between balances in different currencies (with currency conversion applied) |
| Settlement | `ININ/OUT` | Amount of funds withdrawn to the merchant's settlement accounts |
| Settlement fee | `ININ/OUT` | Amount of funds charged for settlement |
| Balance correction | `INOUTIN/OUT` | Amount resulting from balance adjustment. |
| Surcharge fee | `ININ/OUT` | Amount of funds that the customers (cardholders) were charged for (not the merchant). These charges are the internal fees of global card networks for operations processed in specific countries |
| Holdback volume | `ININ/OUT` | Amount of funds held over the specified time period in accordance with the terms and conditions. As a rule, holds are placed on processed purchases by operation.When specifying the time period, keep in mind that:If the time period does not include the day when the report is created and the day before it, then the holdback amount is reflected in full.If the time period includes the day when the report is created or the day before it, then the holdback amount may not be reflected in full (as some of the operations can still be pending).If the day of creating the report is set as the target time period, then the amount equals 0 (zero, as some of the operations can still be pending) |
| Unblocked holdback volume | `ININ/OUT` | Amount of funds released over the specified time period in accordance with the terms and conditions. As a rule, holds are removed from processed purchases by operation |
| Closing balance | `INOUTIN/OUT` | The total amount of funds available at the end of a specified period including the funds awaiting clearing |
| Available balance | `INOUTIN/OUT` | The total amount of funds available at the end of a specified period excluding the funds awaiting clearing. This position is not included in the consolidated balance if it equals zero |
| Accrued holdback volume | `ININ/OUT` | The total amount of funds held as a security deposit at the beginning of the specified time period |
| Net holdback volume | `IN` | The total amount of funds held as a security deposit at the end of the specified time period |
