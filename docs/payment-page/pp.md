# Additional capabilities

Payment Widget supports auxiliary procedures and optional capabilities that improve acceptance rates, adapt the checkout flow to specific business cases, and expose more control over customer experience.

## Boosting payment acceptance rates

- [**3-D Secure**](./pp-3ds) — customer authentication during card payments.
- [**Address Verification (AVS)**](./pp-avs) — cardholder address verification for supported markets.
- [**Additional payment info**](./pp-clarification) — collecting extra data required by payment systems or providers.
- [**Try again**](./pp-try-again) — additional payment attempts in retry scenarios.

## Improving customer experience

- [**Currency conversion**](./pp-conversion) — conversion options and customer currency choice.
- [**Collecting customer data**](./pp-gathering-customer-data) — gathering additional customer attributes on the payment form.
- [**Time limit**](./pp-time-limit) — limiting how long the payment form remains active.
- [**Handling events in Payment Widget**](./pp-ui-monitoring) — front-end event monitoring for embedded Payment Widget flows.

## Supporting specific scenarios

- [**Payment method availability**](./pp-availability-info) — showing only relevant methods.
- [**Saved payment data**](./pp-saved-data) — reusing stored payment instruments.
- [**Payment by token**](./pp-payment-by-token) — shortening checkout for returning customers.
- [**Debt repayments**](./pp-debt-repayments) — specialised scenarios for loan and debt payments.

## Scope

Use this section when the default payment flow is not enough and you need extra procedures, data collection, or flow-control options.
